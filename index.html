PM Surya Ghar Subsidy — Real Details

PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana (Feb 2024) gives residential consumers up to ₹78,000 for 3 kW systems, credited directly to your bank account after DISCOM approval. We manage the entire portal process.

⛅

Works on Cloudy Days Too

Panels generate 40–60% output on overcast days. Delhi NCR & Haryana average 4.5–5.5 peak sun hours daily per IMD data — making solar economically viable year-round in this region.

🔧

Warranties: Honest Breakdown

25-year performance warranty = minimum 80% output at end of life. Inverter: 5–10 years by brand. Physical damage is home insurance, not panel warranty. Our AMC covers all routine maintenance.

📏

Not Every Roof Is Suitable

South/west-facing RCC rooftops with minimal shading work best. Asbestos or heavily shaded roofs may not be suitable. Our engineer tells you honestly — we won’t sell you a system that won’t perform.

📋

Net Metering in Haryana & Delhi

DHBVN, UHBVN, BSES, BYPL, TPDDL all support net metering. Excess units credited on your next bill. Application takes 15–30 days after commissioning. We submit and follow up on your behalf.

⏱️

Realistic Timeline

Physical installation: 1–2 days for home systems. Full process (survey to net meter): 2–4 weeks. You get a clear written schedule before any work begins — no surprises, no delays hidden from you.
