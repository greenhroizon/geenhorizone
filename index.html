Government Subsidy — What's Actually Available

Under PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana (launched Feb 2024), residential consumers can claim up to ₹78,000 for systems up to 3 kW. We assist with complete portal registration and documentation.

☁️

How Solar Performs on Cloudy Days

Solar panels work on diffuse light, not just direct sunlight. On overcast days, a well-installed system typically generates 40–60% of peak output. Delhi NCR & Haryana average 4.5–5.5 peak sun hours daily (IMD data).

🔧

Warranties: What They Cover & What They Don't

Tier-1 panels carry a 25-year performance warranty guaranteeing at least 80% output at end of life. Inverters: 5–10 years. Physical damage from weather is covered under home insurance, not the panel warranty.

📐

Not Every Roof Is Ideal — Here's What Matters

Solar works best on south/west-facing RCC rooftops with minimal shading 9am–4pm. Our engineer assesses this honestly during the free site survey. We won't recommend a system if your roof isn't suitable.

📋

Net Metering — Selling Power Back to the Grid

Haryana (DHBVN/UHBVN) and Delhi (BSES/BYPL/TPDDL) support net metering. Excess units are credited on your next bill. Application takes 15–30 days post-commissioning — we handle the entire process.

⏱️

Realistic Installation Timeline

Physical installation takes 1–2 working days for a home system. The complete process — survey, design, procurement, installation, net meter — typically takes 2–4 weeks. We give a clear schedule upfront.
